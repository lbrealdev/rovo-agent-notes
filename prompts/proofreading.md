# Proofreading Prompts

Prompts for proofreading and improving messages.

**Use when:** You need to polish a message before sending it to customers or colleagues.

---

## General Proofreading

Clear, natural English with preserved meaning.

```text
Please proofread and improve the following message:
- Make it clear, concise, and professional.
- Keep the technical meaning exactly the same.
- Keep the tone [friendly / neutral / formal].
- Suggest 1-2 alternative phrasings if useful.

Here is the text:
"""
<paste my message here>
"""
```

---

## Client Messages (<PROJECT>/AWS Context)

Rewrite messages to clients with accurate technical details.

```text
Please rewrite the following message I'm sending to a client about [AWS/Jira]:
- Keep all technical details accurate.
- Improve clarity and flow.
- Use a polite, confident, professional tone.
- Keep it reasonably short and easy to read.

Here is the text:
"""
<paste my message here>
"""
```
